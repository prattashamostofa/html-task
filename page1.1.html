শেষের কবিতা

by Rabindranath Tagore

Shesher Kabita (Bengali: শেষের কবিতা) is a novel by Rabindranath Tagore.
The novel was serialised in 1928, from Bhadra to Choitro in the magazine Probashi,
and was published in book form the following year.
It has been transleted into English as
The Last Poem (translator Anandita Mukhopadhyay) and
Farewell song (translator Radha Chakravarty).
